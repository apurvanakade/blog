Poor Things
Filed under Movies, January 8, 2024.

After watching the trailer, I was very reluctant to watch Poor Things. But one boring Saturday I had nothing else to do so I watched it in a theater and am I glad I did. What a visual feast! There was not a dull moment in the movie. It felt like I would miss something important if I blinked. Emma Watson and Mark Ruffalo are an absolute delight to watch. Now I need to watch other movies by this genius director.
