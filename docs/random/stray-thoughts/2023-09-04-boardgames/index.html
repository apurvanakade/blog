Board Games in Baltimore
Filed under Stray Thoughts, September 4, 2023.

I have been trying out some board games groups around Baltimore. Sadly I could not find any in the city itself but I found quite a few in the surrounding areas. I have been to two events of far and already learned quite a few new games.

  • Quiddler
  • Coup
  • Wavelength
  • Karma
  • Fluxx
  • Monopoly Deal

They were all quick games and fun to play. If I were to buy any of these then it would be Wavelength. All the others were card games and fun to play with people who have never played board games before.
